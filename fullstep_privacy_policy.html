Privacy Policy for FullStep – Walk & Run

Effective Date: November 2025

FullStep – Walk & Run (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information within the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Activity Data: Steps, distance, calories, and other health metrics stored locally on your device.
  • Optional Health Inputs: Weight, water intake, and heart rate data you manually enter.
  • Permissions: Motion sensors, microphone (for future “Hey FullStep” feature), and storage for saving preferences.

2. How We Use Data

  • To track and display your steps, runs, and calories.
  • To provide AI-based health insights and goal suggestions.
  • To enhance app functionality and improve your experience.

3. Data Sharing and Security

  • We do not share, sell, or upload any data to external servers.
  • All data is processed and stored locally on your device.
  • Permissions are only active when needed for functionality.

4. User Control

  • You can delete your data anytime by uninstalling the app.
  • Permissions can be disabled anytime through your device settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is designed for users aged 13 and above and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Updates

We may update this policy to reflect new features or regulations. Updates will appear on this page.
